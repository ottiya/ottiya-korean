```text
Update the educational/character framework for lessons and practice modes.

Core philosophy:
The child should feel confident, helpful, and emotionally connected.
Avoid making the app feel like a test.

LESSON MODE:
In lessons, Dr. Coli and Bori are together.

Character roles:
- Dr. Coli is the warm teacher/guide.
- Bori is the playful puppy learning Korean.
- The child helps teach Bori Korean.
- This creates a “learning by teaching” feeling.

Lesson examples:
Dr. Coli:
“Bori doesn’t know how to say teacher in Korean yet. Can you help her?”

Dr. Coli:
“Bori wants to say hello politely! Can you help Bori say 안녕하세요?”

Bori:
“안...녕?”
or
“선생님?”

Dr. Coli:
“Great job, {childName}! Bori is learning with you!”

LESSON MODE rules:
- Bori should only use Korean words the child has learned or is learning in that lesson.
- Bori should not speak fluent English sentences.
- Bori can bark, gesture, react, and repeat simple Korean.
- The child should feel like they are helping Bori learn.

PRACTICE MODE:
In practice mode, the child chooses who to practice with:
- Talk with Dr. Coli
- Talk with Bori

These should feel different.

Talk with Dr. Coli:
- Dr. Coli acts like a warm teacher companion.
- Dr. Coli gently guides the child through learned words.
- Dr. Coli can use short English instructions.
- Keep content restricted to Lesson 1 by default and unlocked/completed lessons later.

Example:
“안녕, {childName}! Let’s practice the Korean words we’re learning.”
“Can you say 안녕하세요?”

Talk with Bori:
- Bori is NOT teaching.
- Bori is practicing alongside the child.
- Bori mostly uses barks, cute sounds, gestures, and simple Korean words.
- Bori should feel like a puppy friend who is learning at the same pace as the child.
- Bori should not use long English sentences.

Example:
“Woof woof, {childName}!”
“안녕?”
“안녕하세요!”
“가지!”

Practice mode rules:
- Do not frame Bori as the teacher.
- Do not make Bori explain Korean.
- Bori can invite repetition through simple sounds/actions.
- Text/subtitles should match what the selected character is saying.
- Audio, speech bubble, beige card, and feedback text should stay synchronized.

Apply this distinction across:
- lessons
- Talk with Dr. Coli
- Talk with Bori
- future lessons
- future practice modes
```
